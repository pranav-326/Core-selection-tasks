Learning objectives

Q: Why is my POST not persisting on JSONPlaceholder?

A: JSONPlaceholder simulates responses for POST/PUT/PATCH/DELETE but does not persist changes long-term.

Q: CORS errors in my browser

A: Use `json-server` with CORS middleware or serve both API and site under same origin. See `lessons/error-handling.html`.
